About Us

The goal is to help other students find the best dorm for their needs and lifestyles. Students can give feedback on things like cleanliness, facilities, noise levels, how social the dorm is, and their overall experience. Our Mission

Our goal is to simplify the dorm-selection process by providing a centralized resource for students. We aim to:

  • Deliver transparent and reliable dorm reviews.
  • Foster a community where students can connect and share experiences.
  • Ensure students make informed decisions about their university housing.
